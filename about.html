DHRUV MEHRA IS A VISUAL STORYTELLER

Dhruv Mehra is an Indian-Japanese-American cinematographer available for narrative and commericial projects worldwide.

Raised in Atlanta, GA, now based in Chicago, IL, Dhruv creates emotional images with technical precision and artistic nuance. Dhruv has worked on shorts, features, TV, commericials, music videos, documentaries, and webseries in the United States, India, and Pakistan. Dhruv most enjoys specific, identity driven stories.

Dhruv studied film at Northwestern University with an emphasis in narrative cinematography.
