DHRUV MEHRA IS AN EMOTIONALLY-DRIVEN VISUAL STORYTELLER.

Atlanta raised and working in Chicago, Dhruv studied radio/television/film at Northwestern University with an emphasis in narrative cinematography. Dhruv enjoys stories that tackle big ideas in small worlds.

Dhruv has worked on shorts, features, TV, commericials, music videos, docs, and webseries in the United States, India, and Pakistan.

In addition to filmmaking, Dhruv enjoys cutting hair, woodworking, and basketball.
